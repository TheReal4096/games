How to play: Use your arrow keys o r W S D A to move the tiles. When two tiles with the same number touch, they merge into one tile with same number they summed to one! 2+2 -> 4, 4+4 -> 8, 8+8 -> 16 etc. 1024+1024=2048 . When you make 2048 tile, you win. Only 2% of people can finish this game, genius can make tile 16384. However, someone wants to make 65536. If you make 65536, you will feel special.

Game is based on Gabriele Cirulli's 2048 and conceptually similar to Threes by Asher Vollmer.
